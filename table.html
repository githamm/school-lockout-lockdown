Colorado school lockouts and lockdowns

School districts have different ways to report actions they take to protect students in school. Typically, a lockout is called to protect students from danger or threats outside the building. a lockdown is used to protect students from danger inside the school. The Denver Post collected data from 25 school districts across the state.
